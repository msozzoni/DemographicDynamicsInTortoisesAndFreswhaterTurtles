This folder contains the R script to produce the ENM model and predictio maps for each species of turtle under exam.

The prediction maps will be used in the proudction of Figure 1 and Supplementary Figure 5-22 panel D. 
The avialable area predicred for each period will be used in the PGLS analysis

The presente bioclimatic variable were downloaded from WorldClim (https://www.worldclim.org/data/bioclim.html) 
The past bioclimatic variable were dowloanded from PaleoClim (http://www.paleoclim.org/)
