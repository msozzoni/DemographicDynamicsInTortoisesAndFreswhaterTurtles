This folder contains the R script to produce the ENM model and prediction maps for each turtle species under exam.

The prediction maps will be used in the production  of Figure 1 and Figures S5-S22 panel D. 
The available  area predicted for each period will be used in the PGLS analysis

The present bioclimatic variables were downloaded from WorldClim (https://www.worldclim.org/data/bioclim.html) 
The past bioclimatic variables were downloaded  from PaleoClim (http://www.paleoclim.org/)
